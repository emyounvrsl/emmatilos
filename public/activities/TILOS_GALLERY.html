Photography Stories

Discover the moments behind my captures, the emotions, and the inspirations that make each shot special.

Sunset by the Campus

The golden hues of the sunset reflected perfectly on the campus’s surface, creating a mesmerizing moment of peace. Capturing this scene reminded me of the beauty of stillness.

When there is Light, there is Hope

After a minute of crimping a wire, the goal remains the same—to light up all these RJ45 connectors. A moment worth treasuring.

Voluteerism is the Heart of Service

ain is no obstacle in the pursuit of helping others without expecting anything in return—even if it means facing the possibility of illness as a consequence. Indeed, volunteerism is the true heart of service.

The View After Storm

As the sun dips below the horizon, the cityscape of Manila transforms. The golden hues fade into deep blues, and the once chaotic streets settle into a quiet rhythm. After a long day, exhaustion washes away in moments like this—when the sky turns into a masterpiece before surrendering to the night.

From a Good Day to a Bad Day

If there's a rainbow after a storm, there can also be one before it. The day started beautifully, full of promise, only to end in chaos.

Just Stood There, Watching

All the training I’ve undergone felt meaningless as I stood there, helpless, watching people rescue others and risk their own lives during the devastation of Typhoon Carina. Even now, that moment haunts me—knowing that I did nothing to help. It makes me feel utterly useless.

About the Author

Hi, I'm Emma, a not passionate photographer but loves capturing the beauty of nature and sceneries as hobby. Through my lens, I am aim to share the wonders of the world and inspire others to see the beauty in every moment.
